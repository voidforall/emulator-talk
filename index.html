Matt knows Spectrum well and has hobby in writing emulators. The Sinclair ZX Spectrum was an iconic British home computer launched in 1982, sold over 5 million units. A lot at that time, for a comparison, Apple II sold 6 million units. Also ZX Spectrum games have lots of industry firsts, becoming prototypes for many later games that we are familiar with today. Manic Miner, one of the most influential games on Spectrum. 8bit registers, 16bit pairs, counterpart shadows; IX, IY for index addressing, SP for stack pointer, PC for program counter; I for interrupt vector register, R for refresh register.
